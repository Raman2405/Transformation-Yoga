Our Story

A peaceful space in Charleston where transformation begins — welcoming minds and bodies of all levels. Our journey started with a simple vision: to create a sanctuary where the ancient practice of yoga meets the warm hospitality of Charleston.

Founded in 2020, Transformation Yoga has grown from a small studio to Charleston's premier yoga destination. We believe that yoga is not just about physical postures, but a complete journey of self-discovery, healing, and transformation.

Every day, we witness the magic that happens when people connect with their breath, their bodies, and their inner wisdom. This is what drives our passion and commitment to creating an inclusive, supportive environment for all.

Meet Our Founder

Sarah Johnson

Founder & Lead Instructor

"My journey with yoga began over a decade ago during a challenging period in my life. What started as a search for physical healing became a profound spiritual transformation that changed everything."

Sarah is a certified yoga instructor (RYT-500) with specialized training in trauma-informed yoga, prenatal yoga, and meditation. She has studied with renowned teachers across India, Bali, and the United States, bringing a rich tapestry of wisdom to her teaching.
